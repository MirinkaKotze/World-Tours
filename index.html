Poland

 Poland may not be an obvious holiday destination, but it has a lot to offer. It's the perfect place for a weekend getaway, a skiing trip, or a peaceful beach escape. From impressive natural beauty, to rich history and mouthwatering food, here is why Poland should be number one on your travel wish list.



Ukraine

 Ukraine offers great pastime all-the-year-round, for every taste and budget. You can explore the remote Carpathian Mountains, as well as bustling city life; discover a medieval cultural heritage or find out more about Soviet architecture; marvel at the beauty of Kiev or visit an abandoned Chornobyl zone
